# In Your Language

The most important document for a student with epilepsy, the **seizure action plan**, is available
in several languages, **professionally translated**. We link those official versions here rather
than translating medical information ourselves, so you can trust what you read.

!!! note
    EDAN's own pages are in English for now. For the documents that matter most (the seizure action
    plan and the state toolkit), we point you to translations done by professional translators at
    the Epilepsy Foundation of Minnesota and the Minnesota Department of Health. **In an emergency,
    call 911.**

## Official translated seizure materials
- **Epilepsy Foundation of Minnesota**, seizure action plan templates in English, Spanish, Hmong,
  and Somali: https://www.epilepsyfoundationmn.org/get-support/seizure-smart-trainings/
- **Minnesota Department of Health**, "Managing Seizures in School" toolkit, with action-plan
  templates in several languages: https://www.health.state.mn.us/people/childrenyouth/schoolhealth/hco/seizurestoolkit.html
